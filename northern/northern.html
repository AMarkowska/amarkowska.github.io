Northern
2020-
A typeface I started in Letter Studio class under the supervision of Peter Verheul. The idea was to make a typeface design free of choice. I really love slab serifs and I didn't find that many condensed ones. Also, because I have a tendency to make my typefaces narrow it sounded like a good idea to pursue. This is still work in progress as I only finished lower case which still needs some polishing.
