PowerSpective
2019

A project about how constructions created by humans reflect structures within society. The associations are based on researching amateur and professional architectural photography.

This project is the second part of
a bigger assignment. In the beginning,
I worked together with Aurora Navarro Villacampa on collection of images
that became a visual essay on power structures.

PROCESS

For the first part, we took our own images and after analyzing both sources;
own and found I realized that perspective often depends on the kind of building we
are taking picture of. The grander the building and the more power it imposes the more
of frog perspective we take. It is connected to the amount of space around the building. Correspondingly crowded and poor neighborhoods are regularly photographed from
up to down perspective.

I really wanted to work with space but it proved quite limiting to think about real space.
So it became a great opportunity to learn about using Unity (game engine) to realize my ideas in 3D space without limitations of budget or scale.
