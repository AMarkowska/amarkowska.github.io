Tsar vs Havana
2019
A visual identity inspired by creating a narrative about two artists/star designers/friends in conflict. It contains a shocking soundscape that builds up the atmosphere, brutalized modular posters, an invitation that explains the story, and a cliffhanger of a greeting card.
In this assignment, students were supposed to make-up an alter ego. We took all letters from our names and surnames and mixed them up. Later our classmates gave us a set of brand new characteristics that we had to stick to. I found the idea of making a narrative around our new personalities the most amusing. My alter geo (Tsar Kakao Maswa), according to my classmates is in conflict Havana Rabis. One of the options was to make a branding so I decided that would be the best medium to tell the story. After experiencing and reading all of the elements the viewer pieces out what happened to the two friends that were separated by their fame.
I made 3D models to stand for the artwork made by Tsar and Havana. Their polished look bothered me nevertheless so I printed them with risograph and that became my "weapon of choice". Working with it and editing the printed layers manually I managed to achieve more brutal look. The strict and black typographical layer contrast with the elegance of golden and silver ones.
