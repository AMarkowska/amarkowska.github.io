About the studio

Studio Mesh is the playground of designer Shonali Shetty and photographer Marco Mega. With a keen interest in driving sustainability, we focus on helping brands launch and promote sustainable innovations.

People and consumers are at the heart of what we do. We have a hands on iterative approach where we co-create with both clients and consumers to build impactful brand narratives.
