European Earth Salvation Union
2020
In collaboration with the International Institute of Social History in Amsterdam, we were encouraged to create a social movement of the future. This project was supervised by Katrin Korfmann.
I was really inspired by environmental and peace movements. Nevertheless, although I picked such positive organizations I ended up creating a dystopia when living eco-friendly isn't a choice anymore. China's surveillance and totalitarian architecture of the early 20th century became my biggest inspirations.
Because of coronavirus limiting my access to school buildings, I decided to create the world purely in 3D. I used a game creating engine Unity. Some of the 3D models are made by me, like the bee drone and the huge hive, but mostly I had to use prefabs because of time limitation.
