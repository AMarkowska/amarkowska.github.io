Orange
2019
Short documentary telling a story of the horticultural phenomenon of the Westland region in the Netherlands. It's a small piece of the shore with the highest density and the biggest production of food per square meter in the world... But there is also an alien side of it.
I got a unique chance to make a five minutes long documentary about a subject free of choice. Having many occasions to drive through Westland at night or very early morning I was always amazed by the landscape bathed in aurora borealis created by local greenhouses. I became curious about how the lifestyle of the dwellers is impacted by it. Of course in such a short format and also such a short production time (two months) I wasn't able to explore all I wanted but I have to say I would love to come back to this project in the future.
Full video avaliable by request only. As much as I'd love to I do not own rights to all of the footage.
